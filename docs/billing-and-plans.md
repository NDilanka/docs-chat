# Billing and Plans

Tessera offers four plans. This page lists what each includes and how billing works. Prices are in US dollars.

## Plan comparison

### Free — $0

For individuals getting started. Includes your personal space plus up to **2 shared spaces** (max **3 members** each), up to **1,000 notes** total, **2 GB** of storage, **50 MB** maximum file upload, **7-day** version history, and the built-in template gallery. Community support only.

### Plus — $8 per user / month

For power users. Everything in Free plus **unlimited notes**, **unlimited shared spaces**, **50 GB** storage, **250 MB** file uploads, **90-day** version history, custom templates, the graph view, and advanced search operators. Priority email support.

### Team — $14 per user / month

For teams that collaborate daily. Everything in Plus plus up to **250 members** per space, **unlimited** version history, guest access, roles and permissions, the admin console, and a **90-day** audit log. Pooled storage of **100 GB per member**.

### Business — $24 per user / month

For organizations with security and compliance needs. Everything in Team plus **SSO / SAML**, **SCIM** provisioning, domain capture, a **1-year** audit log, configurable data retention, **500 MB** file uploads, and pooled storage of **1 TB per member**. Priority support with a one-business-day response target.

For larger or regulated deployments, **Enterprise** terms are available — contact **sales@tessera.app**.

## Monthly vs. annual

Choose monthly or annual billing. **Annual billing saves roughly two months** compared with paying monthly (about a 17% discount). You can switch between monthly and annual at any time; the change takes effect on your next renewal.

## How per-user pricing works

Paid plans are billed per **active member** across your organization. Adding a member mid-cycle is **prorated** for the days remaining; removing a member credits the unused time toward your next invoice. Guests and viewers on public links are **not** counted as billable members.

## Payment methods

We accept major credit and debit cards and, on annual Team and Business plans, payment by invoice (ACH or wire). Manage your card and billing email under **Settings → Billing**.

## Upgrading and downgrading

Upgrade instantly from **Settings → Billing → Change plan**; new features unlock right away. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period so you keep what you paid for. If a downgrade would put you over a limit (for example, too many shared spaces), Tessera asks you to resolve that first.

## Refunds

Monthly plans are not refunded for partial months. Annual plans can be **refunded within 14 days** of purchase or renewal. Request a refund from **Settings → Billing → Help**, or email **support@tessera.app**.

## Invoices and receipts

Every payment generates a receipt, emailed to your billing address and available under **Settings → Billing → Invoices**. Add a company name, address, and VAT/Tax ID to appear on invoices.
