# Notifications and the Inbox

The **Inbox** is your single feed of everything that needs your attention in Tessera: mentions, replies, task assignments, due-date reminders, and access requests.

## Opening the Inbox

Click **Inbox** in the sidebar or press **Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + I**. A badge shows the number of unread items. Each item links straight to the note, comment, or task that triggered it.

## What generates a notification

- You are **@mentioned** in a note or comment.
- Someone **replies** in a thread you're part of.
- A task is **assigned** to you or its due date changes.
- A **reminder** you set comes due.
- Someone **requests access** to a space you administer.
- A teammate **shares** a note or space with you.

## Managing items

Mark an item as read, or **archive** it to clear it from the unread view while keeping it findable. Use **Mark all as read** to reset the badge. Archived items stay in the **Archived** tab indefinitely.

## Delivery channels

Notifications can reach you through three channels, configured independently under **Settings → Notifications**:

- **In-app** — always on; powers the Inbox.
- **Email** — batched or immediate, your choice.
- **Push** — on the iOS and Android apps.

For each event type (mentions, replies, assignments, reminders) you choose which channels deliver it. For example, you might keep mentions on push but route reminders to email only.

## Email digests

Instead of one email per event, enable a **daily digest** that summarizes everything from the past day in a single message, delivered at an hour you pick. A **weekly digest** option summarizes activity across all your spaces every Monday.

## Quiet hours

Set **quiet hours** (for example, 7 PM to 8 AM) to pause push and email notifications during that window. In-app notifications still collect in the Inbox so nothing is missed; they simply don't interrupt you. Quiet hours follow your account time zone.

## Per-space muting

Mute an individual space to stop all notifications from it while staying a member. Muting is personal and reversible from the space's **⋯** menu.
