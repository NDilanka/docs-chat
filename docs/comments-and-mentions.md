# Comments and Mentions

Comments let your team discuss work in context without cluttering the note itself. Mentions pull the right people into that discussion.

## Adding a comment

Select any text and click the **comment** bubble that appears, or press **Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + M**. Your comment anchors to that selection and shows up as a highlight other readers can click. You can also comment on an image, a table, or a whole note rather than a specific selection.

## Threads and replies

Each comment starts a **thread**. Reply inline to keep a conversation together. Threads show the author, timestamp, and edit history of each reply.

## Mentions

Type `@` followed by a name to mention a teammate in a comment or note body. The person receives a notification linking directly to the comment. You can mention anyone who is a member or guest of the current space.

## Resolving comments

When a discussion is finished, click **Resolve**. Resolved comments are hidden from the note but not deleted — reopen them anytime from the **Resolved** filter in the comments panel. Resolving a comment notifies everyone in the thread.

## Reactions

Add an emoji **reaction** to any comment to acknowledge it quickly without adding a reply. Reactions are a lightweight way to signal agreement or that you've read something.

## The comments panel

Open the **Comments** tab in the right context panel to see every open thread in a note, sorted by position. A number badge on the panel shows how many unresolved threads remain.

## Notifications

By default you are notified when someone replies to a thread you started or participated in, or mentions you directly. Tune this under **Settings → Notifications**, where you can choose in-app, email, and push delivery independently for mentions, replies, and assignments.

## Permissions

Adding comments requires at least the **Commenter** role. **Viewers** can read existing comments but cannot post. Anyone with **Editor** or **Admin** rights can delete any comment; everyone else can delete only their own.
