# Limits and Quotas

This page collects the hard limits and quotas in Tessera in one place. Where a limit depends on your plan, all four tiers are listed.

## Storage

Storage covers attachments, images, and files. Notes' text content is effectively unlimited and does not count toward storage.

- **Free** — 2 GB total
- **Plus** — 50 GB total
- **Team** — 100 GB pooled per member
- **Business** — 1 TB pooled per member

Pooled storage means the whole organization shares the sum of every member's allowance.

## File uploads

Maximum size for a single uploaded file:

- **Free** — 50 MB
- **Plus** — 250 MB
- **Team** — 250 MB
- **Business** — 500 MB

Supported image formats are PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP, and SVG. Any file type can be attached, but only images render inline.

## Notes and content

- **Notes per account:** 1,000 on Free; unlimited on all paid plans.
- **Single note size:** up to 5 MB of text content.
- **Table size:** up to 50 columns; rows are unlimited.
- **Folder nesting:** up to 8 levels deep.
- **Pinned items:** up to 15 per person.

## Spaces and members

- **Shared spaces:** 2 on Free; unlimited on paid plans.
- **Members per shared space:** 3 on Free; 250 on Team and Business.
- **Boards per space:** up to 20.

## Sharing

- **Invitation links** expire after 7 days.
- **Public links** are read-only and can be given an expiry date and password.
- **Export download links** expire after 72 hours.

## API rate limits

Per token, per minute: 60 (Free), 120 (Plus), 600 (Team), 1,200 (Business). List endpoints return up to 100 items per page. See the REST API reference for details.

## Retention windows

- **Trash:** 30 days.
- **Version history:** 7 days (Free), 90 days (Plus), unlimited (Team and Business).
- **Audit log:** 90 days (Team), 1 year (Business).
- **Backups:** 35 days.
- **Deleted account:** erased after a 30-day grace period.

## Raising limits

If you bump into a limit, upgrading your plan is the fastest fix. Organizations with unusual needs can discuss custom quotas by contacting **sales@tessera.app**.
