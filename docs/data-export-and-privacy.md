# Data Export and Privacy

You own your content in Tessera. This page explains how to export it, how long we keep data, and how we handle privacy.

## Exporting a single note

Open a note's **⋯** menu and choose **Export**. A note can be exported as **Markdown (.md)**, **PDF**, or **HTML**. Markdown export preserves headings, links, lists, and code blocks. Attachments are included as linked files in Markdown and HTML exports.

## Exporting a space

Space admins can export an entire space from **Space settings → Export**. Choose a format:

- **Markdown** — a ZIP of `.md` files mirroring your folder structure, plus an `attachments/` directory.
- **JSON** — a structured export including notes, tasks, comments, and metadata, suitable for programmatic import elsewhere.
- **PDF** — one PDF per note, bundled in a ZIP.

Large exports are prepared in the background; you'll get an Inbox notification and an email with a download link when the file is ready. Download links expire after **72 hours**.

## Exporting your whole account

Request a full export of everything you own from **Settings → Account → Export all data**. This produces a single ZIP containing every space you administer plus your personal space.

## Data retention

- **Trash:** deleted notes are recoverable for **30 days**, then permanently removed.
- **Version history:** kept according to your plan (7 days on Free, 90 days on Plus, unlimited on Team and Business).
- **Deleted account:** content is permanently erased **30 days** after deletion.
- **Backups:** encrypted backups are retained for **35 days** for disaster recovery, after which they are rotated out.

## Where data is stored

Content is stored on infrastructure in the United States and the European Union. **Business** customers can choose an EU-only data residency region during onboarding.

## Encryption

Data is encrypted **in transit** with TLS 1.2 or higher and **at rest** with AES-256.

## Privacy and your rights

We do not sell your data and we do not use your private notes to train machine-learning models. You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing **privacy@tessera.app**. We aim to respond within **30 days**, consistent with GDPR and CCPA.

## Sub-processors

We use a small set of vetted sub-processors for hosting, email delivery, and payment processing. The current list is published at **tessera.app/legal/subprocessors** and customers can subscribe to be notified of changes.
