# Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers to the questions we hear most. For detail, follow the link in each answer to the full guide.

## Is there a free plan?

Yes. The **Free** plan costs $0 forever and includes your personal space, up to 2 shared spaces, 1,000 notes, and 2 GB of storage. See **Billing and Plans** for the full comparison.

## Can I use Tessera offline?

Yes, on the mobile and desktop apps. Recently opened notes are cached automatically, and you can mark a space as **Available offline** for full local access. Edits sync when you reconnect.

## How do I move my content out of Tessera?

Export any note as Markdown, PDF, or HTML, or export a whole space as Markdown, JSON, or PDF from **Space settings → Export**. You're never locked in. See **Data Export and Privacy**.

## Does Tessera use my notes to train AI models?

No. We do not use your private content to train machine-learning models, and we do not sell your data. See **Data Export and Privacy**.

## What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your account drops to the Free plan if you downgrade, or, if you delete the account, content is permanently erased after a 30-day grace period. You can export everything first.

## Can I require my whole team to use two-factor authentication?

Yes, on the **Team** and **Business** plans an admin can enforce 2FA for all members from the admin console. See **Two-Factor Authentication**.

## Is there an API?

Yes. The REST API at **api.tessera.app/v1** lets you read and write notes, tasks, and spaces with a personal access token. See the **REST API Reference**.

## Which browsers are supported?

The latest two versions of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari. Older browsers may work but aren't officially supported.

## How do I report a bug or get help?

Email **support@tessera.app**, or ask the community at **community.tessera.app**. Check **status.tessera.app** first if you suspect an outage.

## Can I change my workspace's name and URL?

Admins can rename an organization and set a custom workspace slug under the admin console. Personal-space names can be changed anytime in settings.
