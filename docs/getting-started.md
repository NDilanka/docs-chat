# Getting Started with Tessera

Tessera is a connected workspace where your notes, tasks, and team knowledge live side by side. This guide takes you from sign-up to your first shared space in about ten minutes.

## Create your account

1. Go to **app.tessera.app** and choose **Sign up**.
2. Register with an email address and a password (minimum 10 characters), or continue with Google or Apple.
3. Verify your email by clicking the link we send. Unverified accounts can read and write but cannot invite collaborators.

Every new account starts on the **Free** plan. You can upgrade at any time from **Settings → Billing**.

## The workspace at a glance

The app has three regions:

- **Sidebar** (left): your spaces, pinned notes, and the **Tasks**, **Search**, and **Inbox** entries.
- **Editor** (center): the note or board you are currently viewing.
- **Context panel** (right): backlinks, comments, and version history for the open note.

Press **Cmd/Ctrl + K** anywhere to open the command palette, the fastest way to jump between notes, run actions, or create something new.

## Your first note

1. Click **+ New note** at the top of the sidebar, or press **Cmd/Ctrl + N**.
2. Give it an H1 title on the first line — this becomes the note's name in the sidebar.
3. Start typing. Use Markdown shortcuts such as `#` for a heading, `-` for a bullet, `[]` for a checkbox, and `[[` to link to another note.

Notes save automatically every few seconds. There is no **Save** button.

## Create a space

Spaces group related notes and tasks. New accounts come with one **Personal** space.

1. Click the **+** next to **Spaces** in the sidebar.
2. Name the space (for example, "Marketing") and pick an icon.
3. Invite teammates by email. They receive an invitation link that expires after 7 days.

On the Free plan you can create up to **2 shared spaces** with up to **3 members** each. Paid plans remove these limits.

## Next steps

- Connect an integration such as Slack or Google Calendar from **Settings → Integrations**.
- Install the mobile app for iOS or Android to capture notes on the go.
- Turn on two-factor authentication under **Settings → Security**.

If you get stuck, email **support@tessera.app** or visit the community forum at **community.tessera.app**.
