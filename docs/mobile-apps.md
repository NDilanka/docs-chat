# Mobile Apps

Tessera has native apps for iPhone, iPad, and Android that keep your notes and tasks in sync with the web. This page covers installation, offline use, and mobile-only features.

## Requirements and installation

The Tessera app requires **iOS 16 or later** or **Android 9 (Pie) or later**. Download it from the **App Store** or **Google Play** and sign in with the same account you use on the web. SSO and passkeys work on mobile.

## Sync

Changes sync automatically over Wi-Fi or cellular, usually within a couple of seconds. A small status dot in the top bar shows sync state: filled when everything is up to date, hollow while syncing.

## Offline mode

Notes you've opened recently are cached for **offline** reading and editing. You can also mark a space or note as **Available offline** from its menu to keep a full local copy. Edits made offline are queued and merge automatically the next time you're online — Tessera resolves conflicts the same way it does for real-time editing on the web.

## Quick capture

Tap and hold the app icon, or use the home-screen **Quick capture** shortcut, to jot a note or task in one step. Captures land in your **Inbox** space by default; change the destination in **Settings → Quick capture**.

## Share sheet

Send content to Tessera from any other app using the system **Share** sheet. Share a link, image, or selected text and choose the space and note to save it into. This is the mobile counterpart to the desktop Web Clipper.

## Widgets

Add home-screen **widgets** for **Today's tasks**, a **Quick capture** button, or a pinned note. Widgets come in small, medium, and large sizes on both platforms.

## Biometric lock

Protect the app with **Face ID**, **Touch ID**, or a fingerprint under **Settings → Privacy → App lock**. When enabled, Tessera requires biometric confirmation each time you open the app or return to it after a timeout you choose.

## Notifications

Push notifications deliver mentions, assignments, and reminders. Manage them per type under **Settings → Notifications**; mobile respects the same quiet-hours window you set on the web.

## Known limitations

The graph view and the admin console are not yet available on mobile and open in the mobile web app instead. Board drag-and-drop is supported on tablets but simplified to a status picker on phones.
